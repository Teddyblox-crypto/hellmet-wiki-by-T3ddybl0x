Versus

Two teams face off against each other in a map. One team receives thumb drives which they have to insert into a computer to win while the other team must defuse the process. Once the data is fully downloaded, the team who inserted it wins. However, both teams can also kill all enemy players to win. Alternatively, the defending team will win if a USB stick is not planted at a laptop site before the round timer ends.

The maximum number of players on each team is 5. There must be at least two players in a server to play. (One player on each team at least)

  • +600XP when winning and +300XP when losing
  • +₱600 when winning and +₱1100 when losing
  • +₱650 for every kill when winning, +₱900 for every kill when losing, +₱250 more for every kill using a hardcore weapon, which are the Hammer and Model 1888.
  • +₱300 for every player difference at the beginning of a round. (+₱300 if the enemy team has 1 more player than your team, +₱600 if 2 more, etc.)

Player are able to votekick any player that may disrupt the gameplay by typing /votekick in chat, showing the votekick menu with the player list on top and the reason list on bottom. The available reasons to votekick are "General nuisance", "Cheating", "Trolling", "Inappropriate chat behavior", "Delaying/Blocking", and "Other". Any false votekicks are a bannable offense.
