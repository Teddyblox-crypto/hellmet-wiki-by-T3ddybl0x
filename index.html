HELLMET is a first-person shooter what was made in 6/21/2023.

Game was created by Migato creations.

It adopts a realistic, methodical approach to “door-breaching simulator” gameplay.

Set about 10 years before the events of Dummies vs Noobs

There are 13 weapons with grenades, flashbangs and even hammers!
