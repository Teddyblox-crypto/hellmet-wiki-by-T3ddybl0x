Weapons

Primary

Bianje 99

A gun that is used by the Dominian Special Forces, made to clear out small corridors with its fast firerate while still being effective at longer ranges due to the Bianje 99's ability ― the foldable stock.

damage: 36 in missions and 22 in versus. 10x multiplier in head

Ammo: 32 in every mag, 3 reserved mags

RPM: 950

Press V to fold the stock

Recommended to have a powerful secondary with it to counter armor plates

Bishen OV-4

A successful Guesan made sniper rifle that was made to modernize their army. The Bishen OV-4 has an integrated suppressor meaning that it is extremely quiet, and on top of that, it shoots subsonic ammunition, making a gun silent up to the point where the guns mechanisms can be heard when firing. This is likely meant for covert special operations mission where not being detected by the enemy is paramount. This design seems to have originated from the Vetala Y88-S, but has been extensively modified with subsonic ammunition and it now has a very low recoil.

Damage: 85 in missions and 72 in versus, 10x multiplier in missions and 1x in versus

Ammo: 20 in every mag, 3 reserved mags

RPM: 200

Night vision: Allows better vision in dark areas. Toggled using V.

You have a lot of ammo so don`t worry about saving it.

N1A2

An integrally suppressed assault rifle used by the military of Nusia and also their standard infantry rifle from 2015 to 2035. The stock of the gun is coloured faded greenish, and it has a relatively high fire rate but low damage. As the successor to the preceding N2, this improved assault rifle introduced suppressors into the production line. Additionally, it had a rechamber button, dust cover, and useful tools inside the buffer stock to fix any malfunctions in the gun.

Damage: 66 in missions and 42 in versus, 10x multiplier in missions and 1.5x in versus.

RPM: 800

30 bullets in every mag, 3 reserve mags

The N2A1 has a built-in suppressor.

Has one of the highest fire rates in the game, making it viable for CQC situations where you can shred your opponent in seconds.
